====== 4 - Divide and Conquer ======
Created Thursday 05 September 2019

**Divide** the problem into a number of subproblems that are smaller instances of the same problem
**Conquer** the subproblems via recursion. If the problem is small enough, solve straightforwardly
**Combine** the solutions of the subproblems into the solution for the original problem
